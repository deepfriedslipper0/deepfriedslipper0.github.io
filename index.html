Pricing

We have taken every possible measure to optimise the affordability of this service. As a small-scale operation comprised of a single group of friends with a common goal, we do not have to pay our employees. However, the creation of a platform such as this one comes with several other expenses, such as the implementation of AI technology to power our "Helpbot," website hosting and domain name fees. In order to generate revenue, we have integrated non-intrusive adverts, which enable us to keep this price tag as low as we can.
